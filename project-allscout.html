All Scout

Founder  ·  Product Design, Vibe Coding

A Vision AI platform I built as a vibe coding project — integrating with existing cameras (Ring, Vosker) to provide intelligent, plain-English detection alerts. Users describe what they want detected and the system alerts them when it happens. Built end-to-end: concept, design, and product using AI-assisted development.

Why — I wanted to understand how AI-assisted development works from the inside — the decisions, constraints, and trade-offs — so I could better lead and collaborate with the engineers and AI teams I work with.
