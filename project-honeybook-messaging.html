HoneyBook: Messaging

Design Director  ·  Marketing Design, Brand Strategy

Led a strategic initiative to test and redefine how HoneyBook communicates its value to the market. The work combined market research, user testing, and A/B tests on the homepage to understand how HoneyBook should position itself — and whether different messaging could unlock new audience segments.

I oversaw the rebrand of the website and worked with freelancers to build and test distinct paid landing pages, each targeting a different audience with a different value proposition. The goal was to move beyond assumptions and let data drive positioning decisions.

Impact — The findings from this work directly shaped HoneyBook's positioning strategy and are now a major track in Q2 2026 — informing how the product, brand, and growth teams approach audience targeting and acquisition going forward.
