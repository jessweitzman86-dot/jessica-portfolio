Meta: Novi Care

Content Design Manager  ·  Content Design, UX Strategy

Novi was Meta's cryptocurrency wallet — one of the most complex and regulated consumer financial products Facebook had ever shipped. I built and led the team responsible for the entire customer support experience: taxonomy, support flows, issue triaging, and the language systems users encountered when something went wrong with their money.

The challenge — Crypto support is uniquely high-stakes. Users deal with real money, irreversible transactions, and regulatory constraints. Building the taxonomy meant mapping every failure state before launch, then designing language that could de-escalate anxiety while remaining legally precise.
