HoneyBook: Onboarding Improvements

Design Director  ·  Product Design, Growth

Managing the Growth product design team, I established design frameworks and systemic thinking to deliver a new simplified home experience and chat-based onboarding, directly tied to trial user activation and conversion OKRs.

The work centred on reducing friction at every stage of the trial journey — making it faster for new users to understand HoneyBook's value, commit to the product, and convert. Each design decision was tracked against activation and conversion metrics, ensuring the team was always building toward measurable business impact.

Approach — Design frameworks aren't just about consistency — they're about velocity. By establishing clear systemic patterns early, the team could iterate faster, test more hypotheses, and ship improvements without starting from scratch each cycle.
