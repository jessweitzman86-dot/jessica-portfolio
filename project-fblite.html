Facebook Lite

Content Design Manager  ·  Content Design, Product Strategy

Facebook Lite was initially built to give users with device, network, and data restrictions access to Facebook without the worry of excessive charges or a disappointing experience. In reality the app was very limited — the interface wouldn't have looked out of place alongside a basic mobile website.

The opportunity was to close that gap: transforming Lite from a bare-bones fallback into a genuinely useful, well-considered product for the hundreds of millions of people relying on it as their primary way to access Facebook.

The challenge — Designing for constrained environments requires stripping everything back to what actually matters. Every decision — language, hierarchy, interaction — had to work for users on slow networks, low-end devices, and limited data plans, while still feeling like a product worth using.
