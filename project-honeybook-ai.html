HoneyBook: AI Onboarding

Design Director  ·  Product Design, AI

We replaced a static onboarding questionnaire with a conversational AI flow that learns in real time what a new trialer needs to commit to during setup, and knows when to scale the experience back. The AI guides users through building their first real business document, adapting depth and pacing based on how they respond.

Before

Static Q&A collecting business data — not helping users experience the product's value.

After

AI-assisted flow building a real document with the user — surfacing value immediately.

Key insight — Onboarding success isn't about collecting data, it's about helping users experience core value as fast as possible. Integrating generative AI let us constantly optimise for commitment and scale back at the right moments.
